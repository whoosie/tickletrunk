Dragon Bowls

A dragon bowl is a delicous and hearty meal made up of brown rice, veggies, tofu, and sauce. It is mostly raw, quick to make, and very nutrient-rich. Let's get started learning how to make one for ourselves!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Brown Rice
  • 1 cup Broccoli, steamed
  • 1 cup Sprouts
  • 1 cup Grated Beet
  • 1 cup Pickled Daikon
  • 1 Tbs Sesame Seeds
  • 0.5 cup Dragon Sauce
  • 1 Lime

Directions

  1. Prepare Rice

    Cook your brown rice in a rice cooker or cooking pot. Be careful not to overcook. Usually a 2 to 1 ratio of water to rice works well.

  2. Prepare Veggies

    Shred beets, lightly steam broccoi, and julienne the daikon. Set aside other ingredients for assembly.

  3. Final Touches and Assembly

    Place a scoop of rice in the bottom of each serving bowl. Arrange veggies on top, as seen in the photo at the top of the page. Drizzle with sauce and sprinkle with sesame seeds.

Your Dragon Bowl is Ready to Eat!
